My Personal Experience of Counselling

Life can be difficult at times, and we may need help to process our grief, difficulties in the relationship, Work related issues, anxiety, depression, anger, Self-criticism and lack of self-belief. I struggled with self-criticism and a lack of self-belief. It wasn't until late on in my life when I began my counselling journey that this started to change. That is why I am passionate about using Transactional Analysis to help challenge the unhelpful beliefs we can hold about ourselves.

What is Transactional Analysis?

It is a theory of personality that can be used for personal growth, to better understand oneself and others by finding more effective ways to communicate.
